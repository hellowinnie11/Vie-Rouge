About Us









Our brand Vie Rouge is synonymous with creativity, elegance, sense of humour and originality, and was founded in Malaysia in 2020. It is one of the leading international fashion design companies and a symbol of world fashion goods/products. It designs, manufactures, distributes and retails fashion and lifestyle products, including high fashion, ready-to-wear, bags, shoes, accessories, jewelry, watches, glasses and all products bearing the unique style. The Vie Rouge designer has been the artistic director of our brand since 2020 and led the brand into the today's fashion world. Today, Vie Rouge represents its tradition through its powerful and fearless design, while facing a new global audience, which will continue to strengthen our position in contemporary culture.
